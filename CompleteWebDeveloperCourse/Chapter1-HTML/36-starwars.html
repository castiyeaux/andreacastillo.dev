Origin of "Hello There" meme:

The above scene is from Star Wars™ creator, George Lucas' final installment of the Skywalker Saga, Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith.
Since the movie's 2005 release, the scene has become a very popular greeting, joke, and meme among fans of all ages.
