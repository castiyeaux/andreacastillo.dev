Common Character Entities

  •   , non-breaking space (since HTML doesn't recgonize spaces immediately after an opening tag or multiple spaces): "&nb sp;" (without the space in between)
  • < , less than: "&l t;" (without the space in between)
  • > , greater than: "&g t;" (without the space in between)
  • & , ampersand: "&am p;" (without the space in between)
  • " , double quotation mark: '&qu ot;' (without the space in between)
  • ' , single quotation mark/apostrophe: "&ap os;" (without the space in between)
